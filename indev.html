Games In Development

The Current Game in Development is "Party Mayhem". This project is being done by 5 students for an assessment with AIE.

The game will be a character based party game.
There will be a variety of minigames to choose from.
A potential feature is to implement those minigames into a board style mode similar to the Mario Party games.
